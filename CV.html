University Education

Professional Experience

  • Apr 2018 - Present: Postdoctoral Research Associate, Imperial College London, United Kingdom

    • Led the team of five students for GAMBUT 2018 field experiment in Indonesia
    • Image processing of infrared images of lab scale and field scale experiments
    • Co-supervising PhD a student for planning and conducting experiments
  • 2011 - 2018: Graduate Research Assistant, King Abdullah University of Science and Technology, Saudi Arabia
    • Designed and built a state of the art pressure vessel and incorporated fused silica windows for wide optical access
    • Prepared 3D-CAD drawings of the experimental set up using SolidWorks
    • Designed a counterflow burner and all the hardware to acquire and store the experimental data
    • Investigated pollutant formation at conditions relevant to gas turbines and diesel engines using light scattering, laser induced incandescence and planar laser induced flourescence
  • 2010 - 2011: Fuel Cell Development Research Engineer, Genport s.r.l., Italy
  • 2007 - 2008: Production and Maintenance Engineer, British Oxygen Company, Pakistan
