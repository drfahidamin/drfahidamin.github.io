Research Areas

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate (PDRA) in the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Thermofluids Division at Imperial College London.

My current work is focused on understanding the smouldering combustion and its resulting emissions. I am involved in lab scale and field scale experiments and I used infrared diagnostics and fourier infrared spectroscopy to understanding the smouldering combustion dynamics and gaseous emissions.

I completed my PhD studies at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology (KAUST) where I investigated the influence of pressure on combustion generated particulate emissions (soot) using intrusive and non-intrusive diagnostics. I used multi-angle light scattering, laser induced incandescence, PAH-PLIF and thermophoretic sampling followed by transmission electron microscopy in pressurised environment to study soot morphology.
